Latitude Analysis - Latitude vs Humidity

Is there any correlation between Latitude and Humidity?


This analysis focused on identifying any correlation between latitude (between -90 (South Pole) and 90 (North Pole)) and the relative humidity (between 0% and 100%) observed on the day of sample.

As you can see from the images below, there is little correlation in both the Northern and Southern hemispheres.

However, further review would show that there is little correlation, it is simply a visual perception, as there are a much larger group of cities selected from the Northern Hemisphere.

The greater analysis showed little correlation between latitude and humidity.
